STEP 2

  1. Work from the most confidently understood information.
  2. Use online resources:
    1. Ancestry.ca
    2. Library and Archives Canada (Federal Records)

      Military records, citizenship records, naturalization papers, and Indian Affairs (for which you need to know the agency / department that was responsible for the community at the time).
    3. Archives of Ontario at York University (Province of Ontario Records)

      Court records, land records, correspondence with government departments, education records and private collections (fur traders, missionaries, church leaders records).
    4. When looking for unregistered Aboriginal descendants – they were not recorded in typical “white” or “Indian” records: Often found in church records. See next video for more details.
