Barney William Swan

b. 1924/1925-04-12 d. 1998-02-15

Barney William Swan was a respected elder in the Dog Creek community, called upon to attend traditional feasts and gatherings and open prayers for special occasions. He worked as a carpenter, building many homes in Lake Manitoba. His hobbies included woodworking, gardening and bingo. He also loved to listen to fiddling.
