Napoleon Lario

b. 1846 d. 1912-07-14

According to United States military service and pensions indexes, Napoleon (Pole/Poland) Lario served as a private in the New York Cavalry, 6th Regiment, Company B during the American Civil War, enlisting on 2 January 1865 at Watertown New York, and mustering out with the rest of his regiment on 9 August 1865 at Louisville Kentucky.

In 1911 Pole Lario appears to have been working as a watchman at the Fifth Lake Dam on the Napanee River (Lennox and Addington County). He received a threatening letter just before the dam was destroyed.
