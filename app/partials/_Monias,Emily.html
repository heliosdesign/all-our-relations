b. 1888-12-19 d. 1975-09-25

Born at Garden Hill, Manitoba and married to Thomas Taylor, Emily Monias Taylor was the daughter of Makα tcewininε, Black Man (Peter Monias) and Nähikwαn´ebikwe. Emily Monias came from a significant local family and her marriage to Thomas was most likely arranged. It's recorded that both Emily and Taylor understood a little English, but did not speak the language.
