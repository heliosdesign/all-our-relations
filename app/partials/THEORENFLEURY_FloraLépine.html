Flora Lépine

b. 1888-09-12 d. 1972-09-11

Flora Lepine married Jean-Baptiste Leclair and their granddaughter, Agnes Marie Lepine (Bell) recounted in the Fort Ellice centennial book that:

“One of her favourite memories of her grandfather, Jean Baptiste Sr., is a song he taught her, "Ouvre Moi La Porte, La Belle". This ditty had been passed down from a close friend, Louis Riel.” (See email from Janet Lafrance who has traced this song).

Through this line, Theoren Fleury is 5th cousin to the Lepines of the Red River Resistance.
