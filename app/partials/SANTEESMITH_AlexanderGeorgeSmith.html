Alexander George Smith

b. 1849 - d. 1933

He was an Interpreter for the St. Paul's Church of England, Kemyengah, under the New England Company, near the famous Sour Springs. He was also a chief of the Mohawks and was described as "one of those men whose desire for education ran high" and at the age of 13 he applied to a friend of the Indians, Rev. Canon Nelles for a position in the Mohawk Institute. His family consisted of six children, viz., Charlotte, Henrietta, Mary L., Lily N., Alexander, G. E.
