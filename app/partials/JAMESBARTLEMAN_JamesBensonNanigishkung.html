James Benson Nanigishkung

b. 1845 - d. 1918

Chief Thomas Nanigishkung's name appears on several early Rama reserve documents and seems to have been a “junior” chief, under Chief William Yellowhead (aka Musquakie). When Chief Yellowhead died in 1864, Thomas’s son, Joseph Benson Nanigishkung because Chief.

Prior to this, Joseph had been an interpreter (on salary with Indian Affairs).

In 1871, Joseph was listed in the census only as a “farmer and hunter,” Thomas being listed as a Chief, but this was crossed out. Joseph’s wife, Sarah Young, was also the daughter of a minor chief at Rama.
